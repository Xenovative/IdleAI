How to Play

Your goal is to build the most advanced AI model by accumulating parameters (or at least convince investors you're doing that).

  • Write Code to generate compute units (coffee helps)
  • Collect Datasets to train your model (quality varies wildly)
  • Train your Model to gain parameters (mostly just numbers going up)
  • Publish Papers to gain funding (no one reads them anyway)

Research & Development

Burn through funding on these questionably effective upgrades:

  • Hire researchers (they mostly browse social media)
  • Improve compute capacity (fancy blinking lights included)
  • Enhance datasets (50% less cat pictures, maybe)
